About Screwed Hardware

We've been providing quality tools and hardware solutions since 1985. Get to know the people behind Trinidad's most trusted hardware store.

Our Story

Screwed Hardware began as a small family-owned shop in 1985 when Elaina and Krisgringo Screwes opened their first location on Coffee Street in San Fernando. With just 500 square feet of retail space and a passion for quality tools, they set out to create a hardware store that would become a cornerstone of the community.

Over the decades, we've grown from that humble beginning to become Trinidad's premier destination for hardware, power tools, and home improvement solutions. Now with three locations across the island, we continue to uphold the same values that guided our founders: quality products, fair prices, and exceptional service.

While we've expanded our inventory and embraced modern technology, we've never lost the personal touch that makes Screwed Hardware special. Many of our employees have been with us for over 20 years, creating lasting relationships with the customers we serve.

Our Mission

At Screwed Hardware, our mission is to empower our customers with the right tools, knowledge, and support to tackle any project with confidence. We believe that with the proper equipment and guidance, anyone can become a capable DIYer or professional.

We are committed to:

• Providing high-quality, reliable tools that stand the test of time
• Offering competitive prices without compromising on quality
• Sharing our expertise through exceptional customer service
• Supporting our local community through employment and outreach programs
• Promoting sustainable practices in construction and home improvement

Whether you're a professional contractor or a weekend warrior, we're here to help you get the job done right.

Our Values

Quality First
